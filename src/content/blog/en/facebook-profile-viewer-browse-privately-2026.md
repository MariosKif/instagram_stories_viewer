### Browser Extensions for Enhanced Privacy

Several browser extensions block trackers, prevent cookie storage, and minimize the data Facebook collects during your browsing session.

- **Tracker blockers.** Prevent Facebook's tracking pixels from loading, stopping cross-site tracking.
- **Cookie managers.** Automatically clear Facebook cookies after each session.
- **Fingerprint protectors.** Randomize your browser fingerprint data so Facebook cannot identify your device.
- **Script blockers.** Disable Facebook's analytics scripts while allowing core content to load.
