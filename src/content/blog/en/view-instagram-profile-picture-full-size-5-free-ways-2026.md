## Why Instagram Limits Profile Picture Size

### Technical Reasons Behind the Small Circle

Instagram compresses profile pictures to save bandwidth and speed up loading times. With over two billion monthly users, serving full-resolution profile pictures to every visitor would significantly increase server costs. The circular crop also maintains a consistent visual style across the platform.

### What Resolution Does Instagram Actually Store?

Instagram keeps the original upload plus several compressed versions.
